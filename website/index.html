Ask the Office

Choose how advisors are used: Auto routes by topic, or pick a specialist — agriculture (coordinator), Farm Bill, USDA programs, food security, commodity policy, criminal justice (coordinator), policing reform, courts & sentencing, economic, education, environment, foreign relations, healthcare, housing, immigration, infrastructure, military (structure & demographics), national security, technology, transportation, veterans — or the general assistant.

Auto uses specialists when appropriate. Pick an advisor to focus that role for your next message. Any advisor can show bar, line, or pie charts — ask in plain English (e.g. “bar chart of…”, “data visualization of…”) — no special codes needed.
